How does exempting public members of an organization work?

If an organization's name is added to the exemption list, then members of this organization will not be required to sign the CLA. Note that private memberships will not be detected because the service queries the public members of the organization.

Why exempt public members of an organization?

We see at least two use cases you may want to do so:
  • You want your own organization members to not have to sign a CLA.
  • You have a (Corporate) CLA in place with another organization and allowing their organization ensures that the individuals don't have to sign a CLA.
